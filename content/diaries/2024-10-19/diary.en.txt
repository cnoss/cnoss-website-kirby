Title: October 2024

----

Text:

Fall is here and with it the new semester. In [Screen Design](https://th-koeln.github.io/mi-bachelor-screendesign/) we have a double semester, as students from the first and third semester are on board. I've adapted the [framework conditions for final theses](https://cnoss.github.io/thesis/), which I'm running, a little. This has worked quite well so far and provides significantly more [insights into the students' work](https://cnoss.github.io/thesis/works/-explorative-konzeption-und-implementierung-einer-web-basierten-plattform-zur-musikalischen-echtzeit-kollaboration-an-modular-synthesizern-august-5-2024-12-00-am.html).

In addition to further developments for our regular customers, we are currently revising the websites of [Viebahn Systemtechnik](https://www.viebahn.com) and [Basecamp Iceland](https://www.basecampiceland.is). As always: not too much boredom.
